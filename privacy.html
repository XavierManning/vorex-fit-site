PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vorex Health LLC ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Vorex iOS app, the vorex.fit website, or any related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Vorex:

  • Account information — name, email address, profile photo
  • Health and fitness data — workouts, nutrition entries, body measurements, Apple HealthKit data (with your permission)
  • Team data — team membership, role, leaderboard scores, coach-assigned workouts
  • Payment information — handled exclusively by Stripe; we never see or store your full card number
  • Usage data — basic device info, anonymized analytics events for product improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core Service: workouts, nutrition tracking, team coaching, leaderboard rankings
  • To send transactional emails: subscription confirmations, team setup codes, password resets
  • To improve the product through anonymized usage patterns
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal data. We do not show third-party advertising in Vorex.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) infrastructure with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth using JWT-based sessions. Photo uploads are stored in Supabase Storage with restricted access controls.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Supabase — database, auth, file storage
  • Passio — food image recognition and nutrition data
  • Apple HealthKit — workout and biometric data (only with your explicit permission)
  • AWS Rekognition — automated photo moderation

Each provider has its own privacy practices; we recommend reviewing their policies if you have specific concerns.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing xavier@vorex.fit. Account deletion removes your personal data from our active systems within 30 days; backups are purged on a rolling 90-day schedule.

6. Children's Privacy

Vorex is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email and via in-app notice.

8. Contact

Questions or requests? Contact us at xavier@vorex.fit.
